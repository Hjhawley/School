What other car detailing websites do well is incorporate Google reviews and include before-and-after photos.

However, they also tend to use unappealing and cheap-looking colors, and they also poorly utilize space. The information is not very compact and the user has to scroll quite a ways just to find basic information.

I could improve on their example by fitting all of the most necessary information onto the screen without creating clutter. I will also use a more reserved, clean, professional color palette. I will use photos to grab the user's attention rather than loud colors.
